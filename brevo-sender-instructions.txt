INSTRUCTIONS TO CHANGE SENDER NAME IN BREVO
===========================================

To change from "ODP Commerce" to "Larger Than Life Comics":

1. Log into your Brevo account at https://app.brevo.com

2. Go to Settings:
   - Click on your account name (top right)
   - Select "Senders & IP"

3. Update Sender Information:
   - Find the sender profile showing "ODP Commerce"
   - Click "Edit" or "Modify"
   - Change the "From Name" field to: Larger Than Life Comics
   - Keep your existing "From Email" address
   - Save changes
